Our   Specialities

Dinner

Grilled Filet Mignon: Tender, juicy beef cooked to the desired doneness.
Lobster Risotto: Creamy Arborio rice cooked with a flavorful lobster.
Pesto-Crusted Salmon: Fresh salmon fillet coated with a vibrant basil pesto crust, oven-roasted to flaky perfection.

Breakfast

Eggs Benedict: Classic breakfast dish featuring poached eggs served on toasted English muffins, topped with Canadian bacon or smoked salmon.
Stuffed French Toast: Thick slices of brioche or bread stuffed with fillings like Nutella, cream cheese, or fresh berries.

Ice Shakes

Cookies and Cream Shake: Creamy vanilla ice cream blended with chunks of chocolate topped with whipped cream and crushed cookies.
Mint Chocolate Chip Shake: Refreshing mint ice cream blended with chocolate chips and a hint of mint extract.

Desserts

Molten Lava Cake: A rich and decadent chocolate cake with a gooey, melted chocolate center.
Crème Brûlée: A classic French dessert consisting of a smooth and creamy vanilla custard topped with a caramelized sugar crust.
